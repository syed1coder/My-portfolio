About Me

Driven by curiosity,
secured by knowledge.

I'm a first-year MCA student specializing in Computer Science at MS Ramaiah University of Technology, Bengaluru. My journey began with a BSc in Computer Science & Mathematics from DVS College of Arts & Science — where I fell in love with both the mathematical rigor of algorithms and the adversarial creativity of cybersecurity.

I build tools that matter: network scanners, SIEM pipelines, intrusion detection systems, and web applications. I believe great security engineering starts with deep curiosity about how systems break — then building them better.

Outside the terminal, I'm continuously upskilling through certifications from Google, Cisco, and ISC2, and contributing to real-world projects that sharpen both my offensive and defensive security instincts.
